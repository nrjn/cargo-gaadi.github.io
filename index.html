What is Cargo Gaadi?

Cargo Gaadi is a locally manufactured cargo bike. Inspired by proven European designs but built for Indian conditions and budgets. Perfect for deliveries, small businesses, or personal cargo needs.

Specifications

  • Total Length: 2500 mm
  • Frame Width: 450 mm
  • Bike Weight: 21 kg
  • Load Capacity: 180 kg (including rider)
  • Gearing: Internal or External
  • Frame Material: Heavy-duty Steel
  • Cargo Box: Customizable
  • Wheel Size: 20" Front, 26" Rear

Perfect For

  • E-commerce Delivery

  • Local Vendors

    Transport goods to markets

  • Shop Owners
